Roomi, the safe way to find and keep a roommate.

Introduction

New York City has been deemed as a land of opportunity and adventure for ages. The lifestyle is fast, booming, and often unpredictable. While we can’t solve all of New York City’s mysteries, we have cracked one of the largest of them all- housing. Roomi takes the guesswork out of searching for a roommate by using fine-tuned algorythyms to match you with the roommate of your dreams. We aim to create a safe and sound environment for young professionals to fulfill their roommate and housing needs without having to worry about their security. Roomi allows you to life your life without having to stress about the where and how.

Opportunity

Finding a room let alone a roommate in New York City is a daunting task. Roomi does the heavy lifting for you by offering a clutter-free, drama-free roommate and apartment searching experience with no smoke in mirrors landlords or tenants. All of our properties and users are verified using Facebook and LinkedIn to ensure our users safety. We also provide our users with a stress-free renting experience with hassle-free rent payments through Roomi. We’re your one stop shop for all your housing purposes.

Solution







Rating System

Our app allows you to rate past roommates and apartments in order to keep a record of our users’ history. What does this mean? This holds all of our apartment seekers and landlords accountable to providing an easy, comfortable and painless renting experience.

In-App Payments

Rushing to the post office to get your rent in on time and hefty late fees are a thing of the past with our in-app rent payment feature that allows you to send your rent directly to your landlord. In order to ensure security, all of our landlords are required to pass a background check and well as Facebook and LinkedIn verification.

Intuitive Matches

Each of our users completes a personality quiz in order to generate potential roommate suggestions that are entirely tailored to them. We eliminate the tedious search for a roommate by doing the weeding out for you.

Design Process

In order to create a design that addressed the needs of our users, we first asked questions to find where existing platforms in the market were lacking, define our user and brand, and discover how our design could provide a solution.

We found that the services that offer aid in finding a roommate and apartment lack the customization, security and simplicity that our users desire. In order to address these issues, we created a persona of our ideal consumer and followed their journey of finding an apartment in New York City.

Our key findings in our emotional mappings were that our users need a safe, secure and reliable living situation, an apartment that fit their budget, and a place that fits within their desired timeframe and location. This allowed us to expand the existing Roomi app to create an all-in-one platform for all roommate and housing needs.

We followed the previous format of Roomi’s existing platform that balanced copy with illustration in order to create an engaging interface. We chose to build off their character illustrations to maintain the playful and personal vibe of the brand.

Finally, we tested our design with five qualified users in order to receive feedback on their user experience and found that the features that excited them most were the ability to make payments through the app as well as our rating system.
